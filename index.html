Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tours

What is included in the tour price?

The price includes: accommodation in 3-4* hotels, meals (breakfasts, some lunches), transportation, guide services, entrance tickets to museums and attractions, medical insurance.

What is the group size?

We work with small groups of 6 to 15 people, which ensures an individual approach to each tourist and a comfortable atmosphere.

What documents are needed for the trip?

For tours in Russia: Russian passport. For Central Asia: international passport (valid for at least 6 months), visa (if required). We will help with the preparation of all necessary documents.

Can the tour program be changed?

Yes, we are always ready to adapt the program to your wishes and interests. Discuss this in advance, and we will make the necessary changes.

What to do if the weather gets bad?

We always have backup options for bad weather. We visit museums, covered attractions, or change the route to one more suitable for current conditions.
